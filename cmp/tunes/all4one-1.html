All 4 One


All-4-One put together an all-star team for their Space Jam slam-dunk, "I Turn To You." The song was penned by Dianne Warren and produced by none other Jimmy Jam & Terry Lewis (Janet Jackson). All-4-One burst onto the charts in 1994 with their self-titled, double-platinum debut. Their single, "I Swear" was the biggest seller of that year, earning the group two Grammy nominations.
